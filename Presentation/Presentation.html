Overview on Data

-A total of 16 years of data collected along Californian Coast.

-Rockfish were historically targeted by fishing for many years.

-Rockfish abundance increased after implementation of large MPA.

.

Importing Packages

dplyr, ggplot2, tidyr

Manipulation on Raw Data

Raw data orginally came from Raw larval abundance and environmental data for Thompson et al. Raw data includes 27 rockfish speices and 23 environmental/descriptive factors.There are about 10,000 pieces of data in the raw data set. Some of the descriptive factors were not needed for the analysis and were cut out using dplyr. A new column was added using knitr to combine species into a genus column.
