Importing the Data

Raw data includes 27 rockfish speices and 23 environmental/descriptive factors.There are about 10,000 pieces of data in the raw data set. Some of the descriptive factors were not needed for the analysis and were cut out using dplyr. A new column was added using knitr to combine species into a genus column.
